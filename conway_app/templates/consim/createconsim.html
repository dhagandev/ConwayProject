How to Play
Create a survival configuration by clicking on cells to place your character, Conway, in the darkness. Add as many as you like, in whatever pattern you want. When you’re done, click “Next” to see what happens after one step in time, or “Start” to start the clock and see who survives.

Rules:

1. Each alive cell with one or no neighbors dies, as if by solitude.

2. Each alive cell with four or more neighbors dies, as if by overpopulation.

3. Each alive cell with two or three neighbors survives.

4. Each empty cell with three neighbors becomes populated.
